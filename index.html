Our aims to help you, your groups or your organization to learn faster and better and thus to grow as individuals and communities. The initial focus of our website is to help users to identify "experts" in their social groups, and also to query "opinions" on a particular topic on a group.

Our differentiating factor in our website is that we use artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning to continously learn new information from users and uses AI to help users to make intelligent decisions. Our product will be released initially as a desktop website, a mobile app and later on integrations with major social websites.
